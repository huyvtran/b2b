About Back To Basics Mobile Application

Back to Basics (B2B) provides a simple, easy to understand, no-spin quality information about Cisco products.

Products or groups of products (aggregates) may be selected from the app menu.

Each screen will have an "i" button to help you interpret what is on the screen.
